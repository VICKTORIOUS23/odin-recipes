My Easy Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

It's three AM and you're craving a grilled cheese. Let's get off our rear ends and get this craving taken care of! You will not need much to be in heaven.

Ingredients

  • Bread - Rye is the best
  • Cheese - Good ole processed American Cheese
  • Butter - It's 3AM, ignore the healthy options.

Steps

  1. Heat your skillet - Throw a chunk of butter in a skillet, a bit of salt and pepper. No need to be fancy
  2. Toss your bread in - Bread directly ontop of butter and soak in the cholesteral
  3. Flip and add Cheese - Once you turn the bread over, Add cheese and watch it melt. I like 2 slices
  4. Combine bread - I shouldn't have to say it, but cheese goes between the two pieces of bread. At this point I like to press down with a spatula, but to each their own.
  5. Plate, Cut and enjoy - Let's be honest. It's not delicious unless its cut into a triangle. I don't make the rules I just follow them.
